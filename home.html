  • Who can participate in the program?

    Anybody! A core principle of JDI's Analytics Curriculum is inclusiveness. Whether you work predominantly in analytics, are looking to improve your skills, or are simply curious about the type of work an analyst does, the program is designed to fit your needs.

  • I already know a lot of this, can I skip sections?

    Well, aren't we confident. Although attending workshops is optional, it is mandatory to submit the required exercies and case studies to receive credit for the material. This way, the program can ensure there are no knowledge gaps, and it allows those with more knowledge to audit the material and offer suggestions.

  • Can I get involved?

    Yes! Another core principle of the program is collaboration. We are happy to have involvement from anybody who wants to help make the Analytics Curriculum the best it can be. You can learn how to get involved here.

  • What are the core principles of the Analytics Curriculum?

    I am glad you asked. In no specific order:

    • Innovative
    • Collaborative
    • Engaging
    • Modern
    • Inclusive
    • Design Centric
