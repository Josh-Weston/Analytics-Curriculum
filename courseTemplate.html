Data Intuition 101

Analytics at JDI

Overview

Data Intuition 101 is a high-level workshop that provides participants with an overview of analytics at JDI. By providing a crash course in what analytics means at JDI, the workshop is accessible to both employees and executives. Attendees will learn about analytics, what the next steps are for acquiring a deeper understanding, and how the available tools can benefit their roles today.

Format: Workshop
Prerequisites: None
Objectives:
  • Define analytics at what it means to JDI
  • Provide an overview of the technologies and tools available to JDI employees
  • Provide simple examples of when the employ the various analytics tools and how they fit together
Path: Consumer Level 1 and Level 2

Acquired Skills

Attendees will improve their skills and understanding of the following areas:
